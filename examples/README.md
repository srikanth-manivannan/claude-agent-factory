# Examples

> Worked, runnable demonstrations. Examples **teach and prove** (VISION §4, §7,
> ARCHITECTURE §12). They are **not** part of the catalog — they may take shortcuts a
> real artifact may not, and they exist to show *composition* end-to-end.

## In / out

- **In:** demonstrations and tutorials that show how to *use* and *compose* artifacts.
- **Out:** anything meant to be forked as a dependency — that's a real
  skill/workflow/team (it belongs in [`skills/`](../skills/) etc.).

> Don't fork an example as a dependency. Fork the real artifact it demonstrates.
